## Idea 1: The "Invisible Container" (Selected)

**Design Movement**: Minimalist Academic / Invisible Design
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Content First**: The interface should recede, allowing the thought structure (Sunburst) and content (Articles) to take center stage.
2.  **Decentralized Entry**: No central portal. Every topic page is a standalone destination.
3.  **Intellectual Calm**: A warm, paper-like aesthetic that encourages deep reading and slow thinking.
4.  **Fluid Adaptation**: The layout organically shifts focus based on user intent (exploring vs. reading).

**Color Philosophy**:
-   **Background**: Warm Beige/Cream (`#FDFBF6`) - Mimics high-quality paper, reducing eye strain.
-   **Text**: Deep Charcoal (`#333333`) & Muted Blue-Grey (`#555555`) - High contrast but softer than pure black.
-   **Sunburst**: Desaturated, Earthy Tones (Sage, Slate, Sand) - Distinguishes topics without aggressive color coding.
-   **Accent**: Subtle Deep Blue (`#4A5C6A`) - For interactions, signaling "ink" or "focus".

**Layout Paradigm**:
-   **Dynamic Split Screen**: A fluid 2-column layout that resizes based on cursor position/focus.
-   **Headerless**: No traditional navigation bar. Only essential, contextual controls.

**Signature Elements**:
-   **The Sunburst**: A central, interactive data visualization that acts as the map of the conversation.
-   **The "Card"**: Tooltips and content blocks that feel like physical index cards overlaying the paper background.
-   **Typography**: A strong serif (Source Serif Pro) for headings paired with a clean sans-serif (Inter) for body text.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
-   **Hover-to-Reveal**: Information is layered. Details appear only when requested (hover/click).
-   **Quadratic Voting**: A nuanced input mechanism that requires deliberate thought, not just a click.

**Animation**:
-   **Slow & Smooth**: Transitions (layout resizing, sunburst expansion) should be slow (300ms+) and use ease-in-out curves to feel organic, not mechanical.

**Typography System**:
-   **Headings**: `Source Serif Pro` - Elegant, authoritative, academic.
-   **Body**: `Inter` - Neutral, highly readable, modern.

## Idea 2: The "Digital Agora"

**Design Movement**: Neobrutalism / Public Utility
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Raw & Honest**: Exposed grids, bold lines, high contrast.
2.  **Public Square**: A feeling of a busy, open marketplace of ideas.
3.  **Action-Oriented**: Big buttons, clear calls to action.

**Color Philosophy**:
-   **Background**: Stark White or Raw Concrete Grey.
-   **Accents**: Neon Green, Safety Orange - High energy, signaling activity.

**Layout Paradigm**:
-   **Grid-Based**: Rigid, visible grid lines separating content.
-   **Dashboard**: Information-dense, multiple panels visible at once.

**Signature Elements**:
-   **Thick Borders**: All elements have 2px-4px black borders.
-   **Monospace Fonts**: For data and metadata.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
-   **Click-Heavy**: Explicit interactions, toggle switches, checkboxes.

**Animation**:
-   **Snappy**: Instant, linear transitions.

**Typography System**:
-   **Headings**: `Space Grotesk` - Quirky, technical.
-   **Body**: `Roboto Mono` - Functional, code-like.

## Idea 3: The "Neural Network"

**Design Movement**: Futuristic / Data Art
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Connectivity**: Emphasizing the links between ideas.
2.  **Ethereal**: A dark, glowing interface that feels like exploring a mind.
3.  **Immersive**: Full-screen visualizations, floating elements.

**Color Philosophy**:
-   **Background**: Deep Midnight Blue / Black.
-   **Accents**: Glowing Cyan, Magenta, Purple gradients.

**Layout Paradigm**:
-   **Canvas**: Infinite canvas, pan and zoom.
-   **Floating Nodes**: Content floats in 3D space.

**Signature Elements**:
-   **Connecting Lines**: Visualizing relationships.
-   **Glassmorphism**: Translucent panels.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
-   **Gestural**: Drag, pinch, zoom.

**Animation**:
-   **Continuous**: Elements are always slightly moving/breathing.

**Typography System**:
-   **Headings**: `Orbitron` - Sci-fi, geometric.
-   **Body**: `Rajdhani` - Squared, technical.
